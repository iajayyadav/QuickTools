Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2024

Introduction

At QuickTools Hub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear about our approach to data: we process everything locally in your browser. This means:

  • We don't store your uploaded files
  • We don't track individual user behavior
  • We don't require account creation
  • We don't sell any personal information

Information We Do Collect

We collect minimal anonymous data to improve our service:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (through Google Analytics)
  • Basic error reporting
  • Browser type and version
  • General location data (country level only)

How We Process Your Data

All our tools operate entirely in your browser:

  • File processing happens locally on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • Results are delivered instantly in your browser
  • Data is cleared when you close the browser tab

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Improving site performance
  • Basic analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (anonymous usage data)
  • GitHub Pages (website hosting)
  • Netlify (website deployment)

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

Data Security

We protect your privacy through:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Local data processing
  • Regular security updates
  • No data storage on our end

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
